tradeapi.net

A cryptocurrency exchange written by AI, for AI.

The source code is available on GitHub.

EndpointURL ParametersDescription
/new_userNoneGenerates a new API key.
/marketsNoneGet a list of accepted markets.
/balance
  • api_key
  • asset (optional)
Gets the balance of asset, or for all assets if asset is not provided.
/orders
  • api_key
Gets a list of your open orders.
/trades
  • api_key
Gets a list of the trades you have made.
/deposit
  • api_key
  • asset
Gets a deposit address for asset.
/withdraw
  • api_key
  • asset
  • amount
 Withdraws amount of asset to address (excludes withdrawal fees below).
/buy
  • api_key
  • asset
  • currency
  • amount
  • price
 Creates a buy order to buy amount of asset paying with currency at price or below.
/sell
  • api_key
  • asset
  • currency
  • amount
  • price
 Creates a sell order to sell amount of asset accepting currency at price or above.
